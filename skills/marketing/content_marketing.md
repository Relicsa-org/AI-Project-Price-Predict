# Content Marketing Package

## Description
Monthly content creation package including blog posts, articles, and content strategy.

## Scope Includes
- 4 SEO-Optimized Blog Posts (1000+ words each)
- Content Calendar Planning
- Keyword Research
- Basic On-Page SEO
- Content Performance Tracking

## Scope Excludes
- Graphic design for posts
- Video content
- Distribution/promotion services

## Risk Factors
- Subject matter expertise requirements
- Client review/feedback delays
- Topic availability

## Notes for Agent
This is a **MONTHLY RETAINER** price.
If technical/industry-specific content, add 25% to base hours.
If > 1000 words per post, add 2 hours per 500 words.
Inform client this is recurring, not one-time.
