# Social Media Management

## Description
Monthly management of social media accounts including content creation, posting, and community engagement.

## Scope Includes
- 12 Posts per month (3 per week)
- Caption Writing
- Basic Graphic Design
- Hashtag Research
- Community Engagement (1 hour/day)
- Monthly Performance Report
- 2 Platforms (Instagram + LinkedIn or Facebook)

## Scope Excludes
- Paid Ad Management (See `ppc_campaign.md`)
- Video Production
- Influencer Outreach
- Story/Reels production (add separately)

## Risk Factors
- Client approval delays
- Platform algorithm changes
- Trending topic responsiveness

## Notes for Agent
This is a **MONTHLY RETAINER** price.
If > 2 platforms, add 10 hours per platform.
If Reels/Video content needed, add 15 hours per video.
Inform client this is recurring, not one-time.
