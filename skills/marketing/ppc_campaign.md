# PPC Campaign Setup & Management

## Description
Pay-Per-Click advertising campaign setup and ongoing management across Google Ads, Facebook Ads, or LinkedIn Ads.

## Scope Includes
- Campaign Strategy & Planning
- Keyword Research & Audience Targeting
- Ad Copy Writing (5-10 variations)
- Ad Creative Design
- Landing Page Recommendations
- Campaign Setup & Launch
- Weekly Optimization
- Monthly Performance Report

## Scope Excludes
- Ad spend budget (paid directly by client)
- Landing page development (See `web/landing_page.md`)
- Video ad production

## Risk Factors
- Ad account approval delays
- Platform policy changes
- Budget limitations affecting reach
- Competition bidding wars

## Notes for Agent
This is a **MONTHLY RETAINER** price.
Ad spend is SEPARATE and paid by client directly.
Minimum ad spend recommendation: $1000/month.
If multiple platforms (Google + Facebook), add 15 hours.
First month includes setup, subsequent months are optimization (25 hours).
Inform client about ongoing ad spend costs clearly.
